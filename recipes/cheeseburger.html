Cheeseburger

Description

Thick or thin, made on the grill or stovetop, this is the best and easiest all-purpose recipe for perfect hamburger patties every time! These juicy, delicious homemade hamburgers are ready in less than 30 minutes and are a must-make for your next cookout.

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck
  • Bread crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, and spices
  2. Press meat, divide into patties, and place onto baking sheet
  3. Preheat the grill to 350-400 degrees F
  4. Grill for 2-4 minutes on each side depending on size
